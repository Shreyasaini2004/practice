SMART HOME

The term “smart” implies intelligence with decision-making capabilities. A smart device and application can analyze incoming data and make a decision to control or activate a device without human intervention. In the context of the home, “smart” refers to a network of sensors in the house that measures and monitors the environment. Devices in a smart home are interconnected through the internet, allowing the user to control functions such as security access to the home, temperature, lighting, and a home theatre, remotely. By using intelligence and information that the system has learned about the residents, it makes decisions about whether to lock doors and windows; turn on or off the heater, air conditioner, lights or entertainment systems; activate the security system; and more.

Human share an important relationship with environment while coexisting in the ecosystem. Eco-friendly home's value sustainability and are emphasized by the usage of environmental friendly materials and appliances in their design. With this in mind we present you the webpage for "SMART HOME"
